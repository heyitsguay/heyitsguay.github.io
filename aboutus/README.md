#### NIBIB/NIH

The [National Institute of Biomedical Imaging and Bioengineering](https://www.nibib.nih.gov/) (NIBIB) is an institute of the [National Institutes of Health](https://www.nih.gov/). Our biomedical imaging labs work on the development of new molecular and cellular imaging techniques, and use those techniques to address challenges in biological science and medicine.
